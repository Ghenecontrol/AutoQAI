About AutoQAI

AutoQAI was built by engineers who were tired of spending half their sprints fixing broken tests instead of shipping features. We believe quality should be automatic, invisible, and certain.

Our Mission

We are on a mission to make software quality effortless. Every engineering team deserves the confidence to ship fast without breaking things — regardless of whether they can afford a dedicated QA team. AutoQAI is that confidence, packaged as a platform.

The Problem We Solve

Most engineering teams lose 30-40% of their sprint time to manual QA. Selenium scripts break on every UI change. Bugs still reach production. The result is over $2M in annual overhead for a mid-size team — and that is before counting the engineering morale cost of fixing flaky tests at 11pm. AutoQAI eliminates all of it.

How We Are Different

We are not another rule-based script runner. AutoQAI uses a vision model to read your UI pixel-by-pixel, an LLM reasoning layer to infer user journeys, a classification model to triage failures, a self-healing engine to maintain selectors automatically, and a risk prediction model to prioritize what to test first. No DOM access. No XPath. No SDK to install. Just point, approve, and ship.

Where We Are Headed

We are currently in Beta, opening access to selected engineering teams. Our roadmap includes native mobile testing, natural language test authoring, and a full AI co-pilot that reasons about your entire product — not just your UI. We are building the future of software quality, and we are just getting started.
